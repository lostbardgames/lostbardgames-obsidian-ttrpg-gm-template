## Tenets, Convictions & Touchstones

### Chronicle Tenets

> *<font color="#646a73">The moral rules that define this chronicle's version of the Masquerade. Set by the Storyteller for the whole group.</font>*

### Convictions

> *<font color="#646a73">Personal moral rules this vampire holds. Violation does not cause Stain if the tenet was not broken. 1–3 convictions.</font>*

### Touchstones

> *<font color="#646a73">Living mortals who anchor this Kindred's Humanity. Each conviction should have a corresponding touchstone. Losing a touchstone risks losing the conviction.</font>*
